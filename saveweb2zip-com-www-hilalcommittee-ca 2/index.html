Jumada Al Awwal 1446

There were confirmed sightings of the hilal for the month of Jumada Al Awwal on the eve of Saturday 29th Rabi Al Thani 1446/2nd Nov. 2024. Therefore Rabi Al Thani will be 29 days and the Hilal Committee declares Sunday 3rd Nov. 2024 will be the 1st of Jumada Al Awwal 1446.

Allama Ghalib Hussain Akber Chishti
Chairman Hilal Committee of Canada
